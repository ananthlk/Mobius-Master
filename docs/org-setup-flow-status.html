The backbone is done; the last mile is UI. Every backend step is live and verified. What remains: two wiring/gated items (roster env vars; the invite email, built but switched off until there's a page to link to) and three not-built items — RAG's org-document ingest and the two UX surfaces (admin console + set-password page). Critical path to a working new-client setup: UX builds the two pages → email flips on → RAG builds ingest. Counts are by step, not effort — the not-built items are more actual work than the done backend steps, since the hard decisions were front-loaded.
