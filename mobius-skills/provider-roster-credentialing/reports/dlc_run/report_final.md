## 1. Methodology

This report utilizes an outside-in analytical approach to assess the credentialing and enrollment landscape for David Lawrence Center. We leverage publicly available data sources to identify opportunities for revenue optimization and risk mitigation.

**Confidence Scoring Tiers:**

| Tier           | Confidence Level | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| :------------- | :--------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **High**       | > 90%            | Directly verifiable and actionable. Based on clear, unambiguous discrepancies in publicly available data (e.g., address mismatches, missing enrollment records where active claims are present).                                                                                                                     |
| **Medium**     | 70-90%           | Actionable with verification. Involves potential optimizations (e.g., taxonomy changes, NPI assignment recommendations) that require internal confirmation of provider qualifications or billing practices.                                                                                                            |
| **Low**        | < 70%            | Investigative. Opportunities that require significant further analysis, internal data reconciliation, or strategic negotiation (e.g., rate gap analysis where service models or bundled codes make direct comparison complex). May involve assumptions or inferences from external data that need deeper validation. |

**Full Methodology (Succinct):**

1.  **Outside-in View**: This analysis is performed from an external perspective, leveraging publicly available roster data, Provider Master List (PML), National Plan and Provider Enumeration System (NPPES), and historic claims data (state claims data generally). We do not have access to the organization's internal records.
2.  **Location Identification**: Key physical locations are identified based on the organization's primary address in NPPES and additional servicing NPI practice addresses.
3.  **Provider Association**: Individual NPIs are associated with these locations based on address matching and NPI-to-organization relationships, with a likelihood score assigned for each association.
4.  **Service Classification**: The types of services provided at each location are identified based on associated taxonomy codes and their Medicaid approval status.
5.  **Historic Billing Pattern Analysis**: Analysis of historic claims data is conducted to understand service volumes, total paid amounts, and average rates for key HCPCS codes.
6.  **Medicaid NPI Initiative Checks**: For each unique NPI, taxonomy, and ZIP+4 combination, four Medicaid NPI initiative checks are performed to identify credentialing and enrollment gaps.
