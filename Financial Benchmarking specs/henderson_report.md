**What is visible:** Within this $3.3 million, we benchmark codes where sufficient peer comparison data exists.

**What is not visible:** MCO contract payments, facility-level billing, commercial insurance, grants, and other funding sources are not included.

### Outlier Framing

When a metric falls below P25 or above P75, this report does not interpret the cause. It raises the observation as a question for your team to investigate.

### The Four Metrics

| Metric | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Rate per Claim** | What Medicaid pays your organization, on average, each time you submit a claim for a given service. This is your price signal. |
| **Patients per Clinician** | How many unique patients each of your billing clinicians serves. This is your caseload signal. |
| **Visits per Patient** | How many claims are generated per unique patient. This is your treatment engagement signal. |
| **Revenue per Patient** | Total Medicaid payment received per unique patient for a given service. This is your combined economic signal. |

### Peer Groups

Your organization is compared against 10 look-alike peers with similarity scores ranging from 0.8326 to 0.9464. These are the organizations most closely matched to yours across size, service mix, geography, and specialty. Five secondary peer groups provide additional context: All FL Medicaid Orgs, Organization-billed, Similar BH_SPECIALTY Peers, Similar Size Orgs (Medium), and Similar Market (Moderate).

### How to Read the Charts

In each chart, the diamond marks your organization's value. The blue line marks the peer median. The purple line marks the look-alike peer median. The gray band spans P25 to P75.

### Badge Definitions

| Badge | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Strong** | Above market median, holding steady or pulling ahead |
| **At Market** | Near the median, stable |
| **Watch** | Above but converging, OR below but recovering |
| **Concern** | Below median, not improving |

### Key Limitation

Rate per claim measures what your organization is paid per claim submission, not per 15-minute unit of service. Organizations that bill longer sessions will show higher per-claim amounts.

ACT alone represents 24.3% of claims revenue with limited benchmarking data available, meaning a substantial share of your organization's Medicaid FFS economics falls outside the scope of robust peer comparison.

## Section 5: Caveats and Methodology

**Per-Claim vs. Per-Unit:** All rate-per-claim figures represent the average payment per claim submission. For time-based codes, a single claim may encompass multiple 15-minute units.

**Modifier Rollup:** Assessment codes (H0031) combine all modifier variations into a single per-code figure.

**Residential Codes:** Residential per-diem codes are absent from this report; your organization does not hold a residential taxonomy and these codes are not part of the visible claims data.

**Volume Limitations:** Office Visit L2 (99212) has only 16 claims, making its benchmarks unreliable for drawing conclusions.
